To report security vulnerabilities. Please do not disclose vulnerabilities publicly before we've had a chance to address them.

## Suggest a Scholarship

Know a scholarship we're missing? We'd love to add it.

Email us at hello@techsari.africa with:
- Scholarship name
- Provider / host institution
- Application link (must be the official page — not an aggregator)
- Eligibility criteria
- Deadline (if known)

Our team will verify and add it to the database.

---

## Report an Issue

Found a bug? Broken link? Outdated deadline?

Email hello@techsari.africa with:
- What went wrong
- What you expected to happen
- The page or scholarship where it happened
- Screenshot (if possible)

We'll fix it as quickly as we can.
