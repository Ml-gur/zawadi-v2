# About Techsari-Zawadi

**Published at:** `https://www.techsari.online/about`

## Our Mission

**Zawadi exists to ensure that no African student misses a life-changing scholarship opportunity because of information gaps, missed deadlines, or lack of application support.**

In Swahili, *zawadi* means "gift." We are the gift that stands between a capable African student and a missed opportunity that could have changed everything.

Talent is evenly distributed across Africa — but opportunity is not. A brilliant student in Nairobi, Lagos, Accra, or Addis Ababa should have the same shot at a fully-funded master's in London or a PhD in Toronto as a student who grew up knowing these opportunities exist. Zawadi closes that gap.

---

## What We Do

Zawadi is an **AI-powered scholarship matching and application management platform** built specifically for African students. We combine:

- **Curated Scholarship Database** — Every listing verified for African eligibility with direct application links. No dead ends, no aggregator redirects.
- **AI-Powered Matching** — Profile-based matching with 0-100% scores so you know which scholarships to prioritize.
- **Application Tracker** — Follow every application through 8 stages, from discovery to acceptance.
- **AI Essay Generator** — 3-stage pipeline (Draft → Critique → Polish) for compelling personal statements, SOPs, and motivation letters.
- **Document Vault** — Upload once, reuse everywhere. Get gap analysis showing exactly what's missing before you submit.
- **Auto-Apply Engine** — Auto-fill scholarship forms using your profile and documents. Batch-apply to multiple scholarships.

## Why We Built This

The idea for Zawadi came from lived experience. Our founder, Samuel Karanja, watched brilliant classmates miss scholarship deadlines because they didn't know the opportunities existed. Others submitted generic essays because they couldn't afford professional editors. Some spent weeks tracking applications in spreadsheets and phone notes.

The scholarship ecosystem for African students was broken:
- Information scattered across 500+ websites
- 70% of search results irrelevant to any given African student
- No tools to track 15-30 simultaneous applications
- No support for writing compelling, unique essays

Zawadi was built to fix this — not by being another scholarship listing site, but by being an **application success platform**.

## Built by Techsari

Zawadi is a product of **Techsari**, a technology company focused on building AI-powered tools for Africa.

We believe in:
- **Africa-first design** — Every feature decision starts with "does this serve African students better?"
- **Verification over volume** — 100 verified scholarships is better than 1,000 expired listings.
- **Genuinely useful free tier** — Cost should never be the barrier to finding opportunities.
- **Privacy as a right** — We collect only what we need, protect it properly, and let users delete it.
